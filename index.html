Aquarium Shark Cage Diving

Starting out in scuba diving

Your adventure as a scuba diver begins with your first dive. You’ll learn all about hand signals, using a regulator and breathing underwater, and practise buoyancy control and moving around by means of underwater rings. Come and show us how good your underwater skills are!

Discover the underwater world close up

Then it’s time for L’Aquàrium de Barcelona’s main tank. From an observation platform and with one of L’Aquàrium’s instructors by your side, and while breathing underwater, you’ll be able to observe all of our guests: sharks, rays, moray eels, moonfish, etc. And you’ll be sharing the experience with your family or friends watching from outside the water. Come and enjoy our facilities and you’ll discover all of the hidden mysteries of marine animals.

Give it a go and experience something unique!
